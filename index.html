About Us

Creativity | Design | Architecture

Wilson Visions Unlimited is a black owned Company focused on Architecture, Graphic Design and Photography. It was founded by Wilson Gezani Maluleke and registered in September 2017. We have since done quite several graphic design projects for businesses in South Africa and have also worked with Congo Airways. We intend to branch into other fields in the future with no restriction to fields as the Company is still growing and expanding.

Services

We aim at delivering creativity, quality and satisfaction to our clients by providing the following services:

Architectural Design

We plan and design your dream houses from brief to the final product. We use 3-D rendering technology to make you have a glimpse of the final product before it is constructed.

Graphic Design

We help bring your creative ideas to life with our great designs for any graphics that you may require from logos, posters, flyers, invitations, corporate identity and more.

Photography

We capture your best and most memorable moments using our high quality equipment and friendly team to help you revive your moments at any given time.

Corporate and Personal Gifting

We offer the best packages for corporate and personal gifts for associates and loved ones.
